Hi there! I'm Vivian, a designer and software developer based in NYC. I'm a collaborative teammate with experience in multidisciplinary/creative design, digital media, and programming. My passions lie in using technology to elevate art and working with like-minded community members to push the boundaries of expression.

Right now, I'm studying Integrated Design & Media at NYU Tandon and working at the NYU MakerSpace as a TA. At the MakerSpace, you'll see me tending to machines like 3D printers, laser cutters, and vinyl cutters.

In my free time, I like to crochet gifts for friends, make beaded bracelets, and illustrate. I love meeting new people, so feel free to reach out!
